# Curriculum roadmaps

Roadmaps are owner-reviewed structural proposals connecting official curriculum evidence to conceptual checkpoints, dependencies, sequence arcs, and provisional exercise ideas.

They are **not** exercise production plans. After explicit structural approval of a full roadmap or bounded slice, each selected sequence follows [`../../design/prototype-production-workflow.md`](../../design/prototype-production-workflow.md): approve the sequence spine; approve one checkpoint’s precise learning contract; research established classroom tasks and mechanics precedents; approve one or more semantic-fit rationales; build each approved candidate directly as a distinct low-fi catalog graybox; then replay it at an owner mechanic gate scoped to the catalog exercise, sequence, and checkpoint before expansion. There is no fixed candidate quota or standalone pre-graybox comparison phase. Roadmap approval does not approve any learning contract or mechanic.
