## Authority interpretation

These pages establish institutional status and version; the statement text and printed page references come from [`src-per-0002`](./src-per-0002.md). The matrix does not treat the institutional pages as an independent yearly curriculum, and it does not infer a 6H threshold where the official expectation is unbanded.

The common PER is the authority for this batch. Canton-specific precision pages and moyens d’enseignement romands may provide implementation context, but they are not used here as normative Geography or History statements. No canton is assumed.
