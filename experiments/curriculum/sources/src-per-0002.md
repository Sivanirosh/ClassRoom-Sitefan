## Verified evidence boundaries

The document labels the relevant progression columns **«5e–6e années»**. Its expectation tables use the heading **«Au cours, mais au plus tard à la fin du cycle»**. Only selected Mathematics expectations carry the explicit parenthetical marker **«(6e année)»**; the Science, Geography, and History expectations inventoried for this program do not.

Accordingly:

- progression statements in the 5e–6e column are classified `5H-6H`;
- only individually marked Mathematics expectations are classified `6H` endpoints;
- unbanded expectations remain `cycle-2-unbanded` and are used only as directional or diagnostic evidence;
- cells described by the approved Science brief as common/continuous remain tagged `PC-common-continuous` while still applying to 5H–6H.

For Geography and History, this record uses the common PER layer only. It does not elevate canton-specific material or a moyen d’enseignement romand (MER) to normative status.
