# PRE-0008 — Estimation 180

## Why this record exists

Estimation 180 is a direct precedent for treating an estimate as a bounded, discussable commitment rather than an isolated guess.

## Source-backed loop

- **Prompt:** individual “day” pages present a concrete photograph and ask an “about how many/how much” question.
- **Range:** the creator’s range episode explicitly describes lower and upper limits and local/global ranges as part of estimation work.
- **Reveal:** Day 98 separates the visual prompt from video and image answer reveals.
- **Progression/recovery:** the cited pages support estimate–range–reveal and discussion, but do not document an automated retry, hint ladder, or diagnostic error taxonomy.

## Transferable observations

Keep an initial estimate and its lower/upper bounds visible, then reveal inspectable evidence and ask what should stay or change. Whether the true value lies inside the interval and whether the interval was informative are different facts.

This precedent supports the learning rhythm; it does not supply a complete game mechanic or establish effectiveness for the project’s 6H cohort.

## Unknowns and cautions

- The pages do not establish a standard exposure duration or require a brief visual flash.
- No automated interval-quality rule or revision behavior is documented.
- Asset reuse rights were not established from the inspected pages.
