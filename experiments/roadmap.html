03 / Quality grammar

What every pilot must prove before polish.

The method is intentionally strict where the previous batch failed: reasoning action, authored language, feedback and honest progression.

GATE A3

Mechanically distinct pitches

Alternatives must change what the pupil predicts, manipulates or reasons about—not merely scenery, controls or layout.

Not accepted · three visual reskins
GATE BAa

Exact French copy

Every instruction, feedback line and hint is approved for one-action clarity, stable vocabulary and read-aloud sense.

Not accepted · placeholders or generic praise
GATE C↻

Intellectual play

The pupil predicts, acts on a meaningful representation, sees a consequence and wants to revise the model.

Not accepted · reward loop without reasoning
GATE D→

Honest recovery

Hints escalate after a reasoning error. If the final hint reveals the answer, a fresh equivalent case is required for closure.

Not accepted · reveal, then count as independent
